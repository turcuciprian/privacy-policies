# Privacy Policy

**App:** Turn Off PC (Remote Shutdown over SSH)
**Effective date:** June 26, 2026
**Last updated:** June 26, 2026

## Summary

Turn Off PC is designed to do one thing: shut down a computer you own over an
SSH connection. The app has **no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, and no
backend servers operated by us**. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share
any of your personal information. Everything you enter stays on your device.

## Information the app stores

To connect to your computer, the app saves the connection details **you**
enter in the Settings screen:

- Server address (URL or IP)
- Port number
- Username
- Password

This information is stored **only on your device**, inside the app's local
SQLite database. It is used for a single purpose: to open a direct SSH
connection from your iPhone to the server you specify, in order to check
whether it is online and to run the shutdown command when you press the
button.

We never receive this information. It is not uploaded to us, to Apple, or to
any third party. There is no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no crash
reporting service, and no tracking of any kind in this app.

## Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect or process any of the following:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, device IDs)
- Usage or analytics data
- Location data
- Advertising or tracking identifiers
- Contacts, photos, or other device content

## How your data is used and shared

Your stored connection details are used **only** to establish a connection
between your device and the server you configured. They are never shared with
anyone. We do not sell or disclose any data because we never collect any.

The only network connection the app makes is the SSH/TCP connection from your
device directly to the server address you enter. We have no involvement in,
and no visibility into, that connection.

## Data security

Your connection details are stored locally within the app's sandboxed storage
on your device, protected by iOS's standard app sandboxing and device-level
security (such as your device passcode and encryption).

**Please note:** in the current version, the password you enter is stored in
plain text within the app's local database on your device. Because this data
never leaves your device, the primary protection is the security of the device
itself. We recommend you protect your iPhone with a passcode or biometric lock
and only use this app on devices you trust.

## Data retention and deletion

Your connection details remain on your device until you change them or remove
them. You can delete all stored data at any time by **deleting the app** from
your device, which removes its local database entirely. You can also overwrite
the stored details at any time from the Settings screen.

## Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any
information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data
collection services. The SSH connection is made directly to the server you
configure, which is operated by you and is outside our control and the scope
of this policy.
