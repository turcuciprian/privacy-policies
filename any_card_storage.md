Privacy Policy — Any Card Storage

Any Card Storage does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
data. The app has no user accounts and connects to no servers.

All of the cards you add are stored only on your device. The camera is used
solely to scan the barcode or QR code on a card you choose to add, and the
captured image is not saved or transmitted. If you export your cards, the CSV
file is created on your device and shared only to the destination you pick.
If you import a CSV file, it is read only to add cards to your local list.

We use no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.
