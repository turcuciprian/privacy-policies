# Privacy Policy for Clean Fast iOS app

**Effective Date:** June 2, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how our application handles user data, including our use of third-party services.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
While the primary user data and application state are stored locally on your device, this application utilizes Firebase services (Google Analytics and Cloud Firestore) to improve performance, stability, and user experience.

- **Local Storage:** Core application data created and entered by you remains securely on your local device.
- **Cloud Firestore:** Certain application configurations, non-personal application states, or user-initiated interactions may be processed via Google Cloud Firestore. No personally identifiable information (PII) is linked or stored in these databases without your explicit consent.
- **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.

## 2. Third-Party Services
To help us understand how the app is used and to troubleshoot issues, we use the following third-party services:

* **Google Analytics for Firebase:** We use this to collect anonymous usage metrics (such as session duration, screen views, and feature engagement). This helps us understand what features are working well and where the app can be improved. Google Analytics may utilize anonymous device identifiers (such as Apple's Identifier for Vendor/IDFV) to compile aggregate reports.
* **Firebase Crashlytics / Performance Monitoring (Optional but recommended if included):** To track app crashes and performance bottlenecks so we can quickly deploy fixes.

These services are provided by Google and are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google handles this data by visiting [How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## 3. Data Security
We rely on Firebase's industry-standard security protocols to protect data in transit and at rest within Firestore. For local data, security relies on your device's built-in features (such as encryption and biometrics). We recommend keeping your device’s operating system up to date.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates or changes will be reflected by updating the text on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us. 

*You can contact the developer by opening an issue on this repository or via your developer contact email.*
